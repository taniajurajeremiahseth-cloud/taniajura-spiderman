Daranak Falls

Natural Oasis in Tanay, Rizal

Daranak Falls is a breathtaking natural wonder located at the foot of the Tanay Mountains in Tanay, Rizal. It is easily accessible from Manila and serves as a gateway to the region's lush forests and scenic mountain trails.

Established as a flagship tourist destination for the province, Daranak Falls features a magnificent 14-meter high waterfall. The area has been preserved to maintain its natural beauty, serving as a sanctuary for local flora and fauna while being managed by the local government of Tanay.

This is my favorite place in the Philippines because of its serene atmosphere and the stunning turquoise color of its water. It provides a perfect, affordable escape where one can truly connect with nature and relax away from the busy city life.

Learn more at the Official Tanay Website.
